About us

our Core team
and their philosophy

Starting from concerns about the increasing plastic waste problem in the world today, we at Da Nam decided to turn waste plastic bottles into colorful flowers, beautifying life more beautifully. Each "Eternal Flower" flower is not only a decoration but also a reminder of the importance of protecting the living environment.

At Da Nam, we have a great team to help you unleash your creativity and design the recycled products you desire. We help you change your perspective on life with a collection of eternally beautiful flowers - friendly to the environment. Da Nam has an efficient design team to help you plan do-it-yourself product creation workshops in a short time. Enjoy your journey with Da Nam!
